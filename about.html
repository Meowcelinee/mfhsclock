what is this thing?

this is a custom clock for FHS to track the day's schedule and highlight what the current "block" is. unfortunately, this site doesn't display the specific period number, but it at least informs what the current block is, and when the blocks start/end.

i made this because the official clock doesn't seem to work outside of US eastern time. my version of the clock has the block times stored in UTC, and then converts the times based on the client's timezone, before finally displaying the block schedule with the localized start/end times.
